Seaton Valley Android App

Seaton Valley is a local community council operating in the South East area of Northumberland. This page will provide information on the Android App which you can find out more information about in the Guide section below. The app consists of numerous functionalities including pages that are fully dedicated to key areas of interest. These consist of News, Events, Meetings, Information, Map, Contact and Social Media. You may also contact the Council to provide constructive feedback through our Make a complaint page.

Guide

Below is a guide on how to use our app! There is information on how to download and install the app and a description of how you can utilise all the features.

News
Our news feature allows you to be able to read about the most recent updates in our local area.
Events
If you enjoy getting involved with your community then you'll love this feature. By using our Events feature you can view all the upcoming events within your area which will allow you to meet new people and get more involved with your council.
Meetings
Navigate yourself to this part of the app if you'd like to find out more information about upcoming council meetings.
Information
If you're seeking more information about the opening times of the Seaton Valley building or more information about Northumberland County Council's Waste service. Access to that information can be found on the Information page.
Map
The map page allows you to find your location relative to everything else in your surroundings. You are also able to view events, meetings and activities that are pinned on the map.
Contact
Find out more information on how best to contact the council.
Social Media
Keep updated with all things Seaton Valley related by keeping up to date with their social media.
Make a complaint
We understand that problems can occur within the community. Our Make a complaint feature allows you to contact a member of the council who will try their best to be of assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported for the app?
Android phones/tablets that have versions 4.0.3 to versions 8.0+ are supported.

Will I have to be connected to the internet to use the app?
For the best user experience we recommend that you have a working internet connection as some features such as the social media, maps and active hyperlinks require an internet connection.

Can I use the app to connect with other members of the community?
Although there is not a feature that allows members of the community to directly connect you can use our social media features to discover more members of the community online.

How do I access the menu shown on the Guide page?
There is a hamburger styled menu in the top left. Click that to toggle on the menu list that's displayed in the Guide page.

Can I download this on iOS?
Unfortunately at this point in time there is no support for iOS devices.

What can I do with the app?
Your Seaton Valley app will allow you to interact with numerous features. Such pages include a dedicated news page, events page and meeting page amongst others. For a full comprehensive guide check out the Guide page.

Do I have to be a member of the Seaton Valley community to use the app?
There is no need to be a member of the community. There are plenty of options for why you would use this app without being a member of the Seaton Valley community such as attending activities.

Will I have to create an account before I use the app?
There is no need to create an account. We wanted this app to be easy to use and navigate through for every user.
